🌌 OUTER WILDS: Critical Tips (No Spoilers)

Before You Start:

	1.	This game rewards curiosity, not completion
	◦	There are no quest markers
	◦	There is no “right” order
	◦	Follow what interests you
	2.	Your ship log is your best friend
	◦	It tracks everything you’ve learned
	◦	It shows connections between discoveries
	◦	Use it when you feel stuck
	3.	Death is not failure
	◦	It’s part of the loop
	◦	You keep all knowledge gained
	◦	Don’t stress about dying
	4.	Take notes (seriously)
	◦	You’ll want to remember things
	◦	Connections aren’t always obvious immediately
	◦	Your brain is the only upgrade
	5.	When you feel stuck:
	◦	Check your ship log for “More to explore here…”
	◦	Go somewhere completely different
	◦	Knowledge from one place unlocks understanding elsewhere
	6.	DO NOT look up guides or hints
	◦	This is a ONE TIME experience
	◦	Once you know, you can never un-know
	◦	The discovery IS the game
	7.	The game is beatable in 15-20 minutes once you know what to do
	◦	But getting there takes 15-20 hours
	◦	That’s the point
	◦	Every loop teaches you something





🚀 WHAT TO EXPECT:

First 2-3 hours:

	•	“What am I supposed to be doing?”
	•	“This is confusing”
	•	Lots of dying to random stuff

Hours 4-10:

	•	“Oh! That connects to THAT!”
	•	“Wait, what if I try…”
	•	The “aha!” moments start flowing

Hours 11-15:

	•	“Holy shit, this is all connected”
	•	“I need to go back to that place now that I understand…”
	•	Pure detective work

Hours 16-20:

	•	“I think I know what’s happening”
	•	“One more thing to check…”
	•	The ending (which WILL hit you emotionally)





💡 MY PREDICTION:

You’re going to:

	1.	Boot it up at lunch
	2.	Get confused for the first hour
	3.	Have one “aha!” moment that hooks you
	4.	Lose entire evenings to “just one more loop”
	5.	Finish it and immediately want to talk to someone about it
	6.	Rate it 5⭐ and put it in your all-time favorites
